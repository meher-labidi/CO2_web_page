Multi-stage for ultra-low temperatures

Multi-stage systems where CO₂ operates as a direct-expansion refrigerant in the lower stage for deep freezing applications.

  • Optimal for low temperatures (-40°C to -50°C)

Secondary CO₂ Systems

CO₂ as chilled fluid

Systems where CO₂ circulates as a chilled secondary fluid, absorbing heat without changing state.

  • Enhanced safety in sensitive environments
Best For: Data centers, industrial process cooling, district cooling

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about CO₂ and R290 refrigeration systems

Why choose natural refrigerants over traditional refrigerants?

Natural refrigerants like CO₂ (R744) and R290 (Propane) have minimal environmental impact with GWP of 1 and 3 respectively, compared to thousands for traditional HFCs. CO₂ is non-flammable (A1 safety rating) ideal for large centralized systems, while R290 offers superior energy efficiency (up to 28% savings) for self-contained units. Both comply with current and future environmental regulations.

What's the difference between CO₂ and R290 systems?

CO₂ (R744) is used primarily in large centralized systems like supermarket racks, cold storage, and industrial applications. It operates at very high pressures and is non-flammable. R290 (Propane) is used in self-contained units like merchandisers, display cases, and smaller refrigeration equipment. It offers excellent energy efficiency but is flammable, so systems use low refrigerant charges (<150g) for safety.

What energy savings can I expect with R290 systems?

R290 systems typically provide significant energy savings: up to 28% average reduction for single circuit systems and 9% for dual circuit systems compared to traditional HFCs. The variable speed compressor technology optimizes energy consumption by managing compressor cycles based on demand.

Do CO₂ systems work in warm UAE climates?

Yes! Modern transcritical CO₂ systems with ejector technology are specifically designed for warm climates. They maintain high efficiency even at ambient temperatures above 40°C, making them ideal for UAE conditions. Our systems are optimized for local climate conditions.
